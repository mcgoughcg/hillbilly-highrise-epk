About the Band

Hillbilly High-Rise delivers a high-energy mix of modern country, rock, and crowd-pleasing party anthems. With powerful vocals, tight musicianship, and a stage presence that fills every room, the band brings a full-throttle live experience built for festivals, bars, private events, and anywhere people want to dance and sing along.

Meet the Band

Chris McGough

Lead Guitar & Vocals
Chris McGough has been shaping the sound of Hillbilly High‑Rise since joining the band in 2019, bringing decades of guitar experience and a signature style that blends precision, feel, and high‑energy showmanship. His lead work drives the band’s modern country‑rock edge, delivering melodic hooks, tasteful riffs, and explosive solos that elevate every performance. Whether he’s cutting through the mix with a soaring lead line or locking in tight with the rhythm section, Chris brings a level of musicianship and consistency that anchors the band’s live sound and keeps crowds locked in from the first note.

Lawrence "Big Low" Horvath

Bass Guitar
Lawrence “Big Low” Horvath has been holding down the low end for Hillbilly High‑Rise since the band formed in 2019, delivering the deep, driving bass tone that powers the band’s country‑rock groove. With a feel that’s both rock‑solid and effortlessly musical, Big Low locks in with the drums to create the tight, punchy foundation behind every song. His stage presence is steady, confident, and unmistakably cool — the kind of bassist who makes the whole band sound bigger the moment he hits the first note. Whether he’s laying down a smooth pocket or pushing the energy into overdrive, Big Low is the heartbeat that keeps the crowd moving.

Hunter Lucas

Lead Vocals and Guitar
Hunter Lucas brings a natural front‑man presence shaped by a lifetime of playing music around his hometown of Fisher, Illinois. Joining Hillbilly High‑Rise in 2023, he quickly became one of the defining voices of the band, blending modern country grit with rock‑driven energy. His vocal power, rhythm & lead guitar work, and easy connection with the crowd set the tone for every show, whether he’s leading a high‑octane anthem or locking in tight harmonies with Stephanie. Hunter’s authenticity on stage — confident, engaging, and built for live performance — makes him a core part of the band’s signature sound.

Merril Myers

Drums & Percussion
Merril Myers has been driving the heartbeat of Hillbilly High‑Rise since joining the band in 2020, bringing a powerful blend of precision, groove, and seasoned musicianship. A veteran of several local bands over the years, Merril anchors the group with rock‑solid timing and dynamic energy that elevates every performance. His ability to shift seamlessly from tight country rhythms to high‑octane rock makes him the engine behind the band’s live sound. With strong backing vocals and a commanding presence behind the kit, Merril delivers the kind of foundation that keeps the band locked in and the crowd moving.

Stephanie Hannel

Lead Vocals and Acoustic Guitar
Stephanie Hannel joined Hillbilly High‑Rise in 2024 and quickly became one of the band’s defining voices. A lifelong musician from Lincoln, Illinois, Stephanie brings a powerful blend of vocal strength, clarity, and emotional range that elevates every song she performs. Her harmonies with Hunter are a signature part of the band’s sound, and her lead moments cut through with confidence and character. On acoustic guitar, she adds warmth, rhythm, and texture that round out the band’s modern country‑rock edge, giving each set a dynamic balance between drive and depth. With a natural stage presence and an authentic connection to the crowd, Stephanie delivers the kind of energy that makes every performance unforgettable.
